Portfolio

Artifacts by Program Objective

The following artifacts are organized by the five core program objectives of my graduate training. Each objective is represented by three artifacts that demonstrate competency, growth, and professional development across the domains of ethical practice, lifelong learning, community leadership, self-awareness, and compassionate service.

Objective 1 Acquire knowledge and skills to practice effectively and ethically.
Diagnosis & Treatment Plan

Case Study of Crystal Smith

This artifact is an eight-page case study of a hypothetical client with Major Depressive Disorder, including diagnostic justification and treatment plan based on the DO A CLIENT MAP framework. It demonstrates my ability to assess, diagnose, conceptualize a client case, and develop a treatment plan. Each of these skills is essential to clinical practice.

Research

Abstinence vs. Controlled Drinking Research Paper

This artifact is a nine-page research paper exploring the efficacy of abstinence-based versus controlled drinking treatment approaches for clients with alcohol use disorder. It demonstrates my ability to review, synthesize, and evaluate literature to inform evidence-based practice in support of effective and ethical practice.
